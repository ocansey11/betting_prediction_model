Project Description:

The Betting Prediction Model, developed by Kevin Djabaku Ocansey, is an open-source initiative aimed at predicting the accuracy of betting tips on forebet.com. This model utilizes data analysis and machine learning techniques to provide informed predictions for betting enthusiasts.
